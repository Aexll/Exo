!22160!You take a walk around the settlement while being monitored by two armed guards.
Here you find sights that could very well be pictures in a history book. 
Improvised tailors making fur clothes, next them hunters equipped with traps for small game
and weapons for larger preys.

On the outside you stumble upon crops. You surprise yourself by walking along the fields on several
hundred meters away from the village, witnessing the onset of an agricultural revolution.
To the point where the guards make a gesture at you, asking you to follow them back to the enclosure.
